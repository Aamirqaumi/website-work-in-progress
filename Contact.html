From March until May, we will be carrying out improvement works to the Library that may cause some disruption to members visiting the building.
Works will take place in the Issue Hall, some areas of the book stacks and the Red Stairs. Signage will inform members which areas are likely to be temporarily affected so alternative spaces can be sought within
the Library to read or write. Noisy works will take place before opening hours to minimise disruption, however, it is possible that some noise may be heard at times. During works to the Issue Hall, there may be limited access to
lockers and coat cupboards. Members are advised not to bring large bags to the Library at this time. Members can still borrow and return books as usual and enjoy the beautiful spaces in our reading rooms and the stacks. If you need assistance finding an alternative study space, please speak to staff in the Issue Hall.
